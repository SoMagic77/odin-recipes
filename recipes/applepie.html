Apple Pie

Apple Pie is the top tier pie

This is the simplest, fastest, and best way to make apple pie

Ingredients
  • Apples (duh)
  • Cheapest pre-made pie crust you can find
  • Brown Sugar
Steps
  1. Cut the apples into fun little sizes, keeping the skin on
  2. Place pie crust into any type of oven safe plate that will fit
  3. Dump your apple slices ontop of the crust, then sprinkle brown sugar all over making a complete mess
  4. Place top piece of crust, and toss (aggresively) into the oven
  5. Bake for 60 minutes or 1 hour at 400 degrees
  6. Burn yourself removing said pie, enjoy!
