Boiled Chicken

Boiled Chicken is overflowing with flavor

This recipe is for when you really want to WOW your friends and family

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Water
  • Dash of salt
Steps
  1. Put water into a big enough pan or dutch oven
  2. Heat water until it is aggressively boiling
  3. Carefully place entire chicken into the water
  4. OPTIONAL: if you want your chicken to be spicy, add in the dash of salt
  5. Boil for 60 minutes, or 1 hour, whichever you prefer
  6. Remove chicken, pour some of the water into a glass, and you are ready to dine!
